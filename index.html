Sankarasrinivasan Seshathiri

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Imagine Team at LIRIS, École Centrale de Lyon. I completed my Ph.D. at the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, Taipei, under the supervision of Prof. Jing-Ming Guo. I have also worked as a Member of Technical Staff and Research Consultant at Streamoid Technologies Inc., and as a Research Fellow on the Indo–Taiwan collaborative project at Veltech University.

My research spans computer vision technologies: image quality assessment, collision avoidance, structural health monitoring, image super-resolution, fine-grained fashion classification, virtual try-on, and 6D pose estimation.
