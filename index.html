Sankarasrinivasan Seshathiri

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Imagine Team at LIRIS, École Centrale de Lyon. I completed my Ph.D. in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, Taipei, under the supervision of Prof. Jing-Ming Guo. I have also worked as a Member of Technical Staff and Research Consultant at Streamoid Technologies Inc. (Fashion AI Tech) and as a Research Fellow on the Indo-Taiwan collaborative project at Veltech University.

Over the past decade, I have worked on a wide range of computer vision technologies, including image quality assessment, collision avoidance, structural health monitoring, image super-resolution, fine-grained fashion classification, virtual try-on, and 6D pose estimation, among others.

I have been actively involved in peer review for leading international conferences and journals. My reviewing contributions span major conferences such as ISPACS (2019), ICME (2019), SIRS (2018), and ICIP (2023–2025). I also serve as a reviewer for several high-impact journals, including IEEE Transactions on Image Processing (2018), Journal of Information Science and Engineering (2019), IEEE Systems Journal (2022), IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, Multimedia Systems, and Scientific Reports (2024), as well as Multimedia Systems, Signal, Image and Video Processing, Scientific Reports, World Wide Web, and the International Journal of Computer Vision (2025).
